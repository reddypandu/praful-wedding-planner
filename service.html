Ghodi Baggi

Providing you with the best experience

We bring you a complete Ghodi Baggi experience, adding royal charm and grandeur to your special events. Our service includes beautifully decorated horses and traditional baggis (carriages) that make the groom’s entry majestic and unforgettable. With floral adornments, vibrant accessories, and live music accompaniment like dhol or band, we ensure the procession is full of joy and celebration. Safety and elegance are our top priorities, offering well-trained handlers and visually stunning setups. Let our Ghodi Baggi service make your wedding or celebration feel like a regal affair straight out of a fairytale!

Safa

Providing you with the best experience

We bring you a complete Safa styling experience that adds tradition, elegance, and unity to your special occasion. Our beautifully designed safas (turbans) are available in a range of vibrant colors and fabrics, perfectly suited for grooms, family members, and guests. Whether you prefer a classic Rajasthani look or a modern twist, our team ensures each safa is neatly tied and styled with care. It’s more than just headwear—it’s a symbol of honor and celebration. Make your event truly royal with our Safa service that blends culture with class.
