Privacy Policy

Welcome to Praful Wedding Planner. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share and protect information when you visit prafulweddingplanner.com (the "Site") or contact us about our wedding planning services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, postal address.
  • Event information: event date, venue, guest count, services requested and related notes.
  • Payment details: when you pay through a third-party gateway we may receive transaction confirmations; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited and analytics data.
  • Other data: anything you voluntarily submit (messages, attachments, photos related to your event).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide wedding-planning services.
  • Process bookings, confirmations and related communications.
  • Improve our website, services and customer experience.
  • Send service-related updates and (if you consent) marketing messages.
  • Meet legal or regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Trusted service providers and vendors (e.g., venues, decorators, caterers, photographers) who assist in delivering your event — only to the extent necessary to deliver services.
  • Payment processors or platforms you choose to pay through.
  • Authorities when required by law (court order, legal process) or to protect rights and safety.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enable site features and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookies in your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure — absolute security cannot be guaranteed for information transmitted over the internet.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described or as required by law. When no longer required, we will delete or anonymize your data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, or objection to processing. To exercise rights, contact us using the details below.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review third-party privacy policies before providing data.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn we collected data from a child under 18, we will promptly delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will indicate when changes were made. Please check back periodically.
