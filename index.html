About Us

Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I choose Praful Wedding Planner for my wedding?
Praful Wedding Planner specializes in creating memorable wedding experiences with unique themes, royal bride-groom entries, traditional safa tying, stunning wedding crackers, and complete event management. We ensure every detail is executed with perfection.
Do you provide customized bride-groom entry themes?
Yes, we create personalized bride-groom entry ideas with options like royal palanquins, flower showers, sparkler tunnels, music bands, and theme-based setups to make your wedding entry unforgettable.
Can you arrange safas for wedding guests?
Absolutely! We offer colorful and traditional safas/turbans for weddings, available in Rajasthani, Gujarati, and Maharashtrian styles. Our professional team also provides safa tying services for all your guests.
Do you arrange wedding fireworks and crackers?
Yes, Praful Wedding Planner provides safe and grand wedding fireworks and crackers displays. From sparkler entries to sky-high fireworks, we design mesmerizing effects to light up your celebrations.
What wedding services do you provide apart from planning?
Along with wedding planning, we handle venue decoration, bride-groom entries, safa tying, wedding crackers, sound & lighting, catering support, and complete event coordination.
Do you offer destination wedding planning?
Yes, we specialize in organizing destination weddings with end-to-end arrangements including logistics, decor, entertainment, and luxury bride-groom entries for a seamless experience.

Weddings are brought to life with grace and charm—from floral beauty to elegant lighting, making every second truly memorable.
